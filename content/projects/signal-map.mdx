## Objective

Signal Map converts policy and runtime telemetry into a queryable graph of mismatch signals.

## Architecture

Collectors ingest control-plane events, normalize them and emit relation edges into a static analysis pipeline.

## Why It Matters

It gives researchers a stable way to inspect how identity abstractions diverge over time.
