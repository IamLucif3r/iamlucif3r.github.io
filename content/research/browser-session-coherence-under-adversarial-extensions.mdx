## Scope

This work examines mismatch between browser session assumptions and extension-level mutation channels.

## Threat Model

The adversary controls extension logic but not browser binaries.

## Observation

Session coherence fails at translation boundaries: cookie jars, storage sync and credential management APIs.

## Ongoing Work

The next phase introduces deterministic replay to capture divergence paths.
