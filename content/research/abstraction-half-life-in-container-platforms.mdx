## Research Question

How long does a platform abstraction remain trustworthy before operators bypass it?

## Model

We define abstraction half-life as the time until at least half of critical operations route around the original abstraction.

## Instrumentation

eBPF traces expose hidden control flow that metrics and logs usually miss.

## Results

Clusters with aggressive release cadences experienced faster abstraction decay unless policy and runtime telemetry were co-designed.
